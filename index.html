that work in silence.

Each active is selected for clinical evidence, not trend. We publish our full ingredient index — concentrations included — because we have nothing to hide and everything to prove.

01

Rosehip CO₂

Chilean Highlands

Cold-CO₂ extraction preserves trans-retinoic acid precursors and fatty acid profile. Clinically shown to reduce hyperpigmentation by up to 38% in 12 weeks.

02

Finnish Lapland

Among the highest natural vitamin E concentrations measured. Ellagic acid inhibits collagenase activity, supporting structural integrity of the dermis.

A long-chain polysaccharide that forms an invisible moisture lattice on skin. Shown to reduce TEWL by up to 26% and calm reactive skin within 48 hours.

Sea buckthorn harvested at peak ripeness for maximum omega-7 yield. A rare fatty acid that regenerates mucosal tissue and accelerates wound healing.
